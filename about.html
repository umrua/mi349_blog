About Me

Hello! My name is Muhammad Umar, and I'm a student in my senior year at Michigan State University in the Communication Arts & Sciences college. I'm currently studying for my Bachelors of Arts in Information Science, as well as a secondary major in Games & Interactive Media. I hope to progress my knowledge and expertise in IT networking and systems administration, as well as my skills in web development!

  • The Apothecary Diaries by Natsu Hyuuga
  • Michael Vey by Richard Paul Evans
  • Solo Leveling by Chugong
  • Ranger's Apprentice by John Flanagan
  • One Piece by Eiichiro Oda
