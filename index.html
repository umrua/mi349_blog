Day in the Life

Written below is how my morning went today, September 23rd, 2025

I wake up to the sound of my alarm after I snoozed it five times, eyes refusing to open. I check the time on my phone to see that I have to get out of the house in an hour if I want to make it to class on time. I had to do the following really quickly:

  • Use the bathroom
  • Brush my teeth
  • Change into my clothes
  • Eat a quick snack
  • Grab my wallet and keys

In the nick of time, I head out the door and hop into my car.
